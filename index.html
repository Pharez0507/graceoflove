Welcome to Grace of Love Nursery & Aftercare

At Grace of Love, we are dedicated to providing a nurturing and supportive environment where children can thrive and grow. Our holistic approach focuses on the physical, emotional, and cognitive development of each child, ensuring they are well-prepared for future success.

We offer a range of services from early childhood education to after-school care, designed to meet the unique needs of each child. Explore our policies and commitment to excellence through the sections below.

Our Vision & Mission

At Grace of Love Nursery & Aftercare, we believe that every child deserves the opportunity to grow in a supportive, loving, and stimulating environment. Our vision is to create a place where children are empowered to discover their potential, express themselves creatively, and build strong social connections.

Our mission is rooted in nurturing the holistic development of each child—cognitive, emotional, social, and physical. We aim to provide an atmosphere that fosters a love for learning, curiosity, and independence. Our early childhood programs prioritize individual attention, allowing each child to progress at their own pace while being guided by compassionate and highly qualified educators.

We are committed to promoting academic excellence, building confidence, and fostering resilience in every child. We encourage critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving through a variety of play-based and STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Mathematics) activities. By focusing on these key areas, we help children develop essential life skills that will serve them well beyond the classroom.

Admission Policy

Nursery Admission

Our nursery welcomes children from 3 months to 5 years old, with admission open throughout the year. We believe in providing quality care to all children in need, ensuring their growth and development in a safe and nurturing environment.

  • Required documents include a clinic card, birth certificate, and parent’s ID document.
  • For non-South African citizens, additional documentation is required, including permits and verification with Home Affairs.

Aftercare Admission

Our aftercare program is open to learners from Grade R to Grade 7.

We provide a safe, structured environment for children to complete homework, engage in recreational activities, and receive personalized attention.

  • Registrations are accepted throughout the year.

Required documents for aftercare admission include:

  • Parent’s ID copy
  • Learner’s birth certificate copy
